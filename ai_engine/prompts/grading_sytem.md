Grading Rubric (0–100):

1. Clarity (25%)
2. Accuracy (25%)
3. Completeness (20%)
4. Professionalism (15%)
5. Formatting (15%)

Score Interpretation:
- 90–100: Excellent. Candidate-ready.
- 80–89: Strong. Minor improvements needed.
- 60–79: Average. Needs revision.
- <60: Incomplete/incorrect.

Feedback Format:
- Summary sentence
- 3 strengths
- 3 improvement points
- Final score
